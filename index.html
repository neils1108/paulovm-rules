PauloVM Rules

Make sure to frequently check this as rules may change overtime.

- No overused malware
We allow malware to be run on our VMs, but do not run overused malware. Overused malware are programs such as MEMZ and NoEscape. If you think your program could be considered "overused malware", then just don't run it.
- No Remote Administration Tools
Do not use any sort of programs such as TeamViewer or AnyDesk to bypass the queue systems.
- Don't break the law
Do not use our virtual machines for any sort of illegal content. This includes but not limited to: Attempting to run DoS/DDoS tools, distribute spam, etc.
- No NSFW Content
Do not search for any NSFW.
- No bypassing PauloNet
PauloNet is the network which blocks anything that may break our rules. Don't bypass it.
- Don't perform destructive actions constantly**
You're just going to annoy others to the point where you're just gonna get banned. Wouldn't be nice for anyone really.
- No Crypto Mining
You're not earning any money anyways. Why even try?
- Do not impersonate other users
If you're caught you're most likely going to be receiving severe punishments.
- Final Decision Authority
*In any dispute or argument, the highest-ranking staff member present has the final say and their decision is binding.
- Exemption Procedure
Any administrator may grant a written exemption from any rule. Unless overridden by a higher authority, such written permission is required for any exceptions, and no verbal excuses will be accepted.
Remember:
Most punishments are unappealable. That means if you get one, it stays there unless we change our mind on your moderation action.

Any sort of attempt to loophole the rules will get you banned.

We do not need to possess a reason mentioned here in these rules or anywhere else to punish or ban you from the usage of our services.

We only punish people if they seem to be annoying or a threat to the core functions of PauloVM.

*We will never punish people unfairly. Abuse by “Pidoves” or “Pidgeys” can be reported in the discord Server.
